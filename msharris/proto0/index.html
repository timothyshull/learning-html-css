MICHAEL SEAN HARRIS

For several years since 1991 Michael had been a member of The Little People and Teen Players Club and a founding member of Ashe Caribbean Performing Arts Ensemble (Both are performing arts ensembles in Jamaica). While a member of these groups, Michael performed an eclectic mix of musical and theatrical styles and toured the U.S., the Caribbean and the UK. Apart from the training in Acting, movement and choral singing which he received as a member of these groups Michael studied piano, voice and viola privately.
